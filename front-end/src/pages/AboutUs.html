About Us

In this project we chose the states West Virginia, Pennsylvania, and Maryland. These specific states are very important to us because it is all of our hometowns! This project overview is called GrowZone it is a website we created to connect people with shared interests in plants native to the specific areas. With this discover information about the local plants, with their specific name and their family classification. The platform also allows users to create profiles and groups to share plant photos with others in their state.
